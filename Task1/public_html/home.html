Who are we?

Sourukirā is a game development company with roughly around 100 members on our team. We are the designers behind games like Zack and Zack's Rebirth. We are located in Montreal Canada. We serve by our goal: For the gamers, by the gamers and only create games for gamers, for non-gamers, for everyone. We promise games that will revolutionize the gaming industry!

What does Sourukirā mean?

Sourukirā's loose translation is Soul Killer, which was Adriaan Rossouw's first gamertag in the olden days. The love for anime and Japanese culture was the reason behind the Japanese translated Soul Killer. The team agreed that the game company should stick to it's roots by creating games close to the CEO's heart since Sourukirā is a game development company that creates games for the people and not for the money

About Sourukirā

Sourukira was found by Adriaan Rossouw in 2026 when he invested most of his earnings in a building that would later become the HQ of the game Developement company, Sourukira. Sourukirā formerly had the name Rogue Game Development but when the company grew bigger, the company decided together that the rebrand name would be Sourukirā.
